Privacy Policy for ChannelSense TON Bot

Last updated: July 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Our bot collects the following information:

  • Telegram user ID and username
  • Messages sent in groups where the bot is present
  • TON wallet addresses (when connected voluntarily)
  • Usage statistics and analytics data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide channel analytics and insights
  • Calculate user engagement scores
  • Distribute NFT rewards to active participants
  • Generate sentiment analysis reports

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database and is not shared with third parties except as necessary for bot functionality (AI analysis, blockchain transactions).

4. Your Rights

You can request data deletion by contacting the bot administrator.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: @channelsense_support
